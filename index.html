CFF Pattern Interpretation

CFF decomposes the essay into eight indicators that capture argument structure, transitions, revision depth and diversity of evidence.

In this profile, high HI together with RDX is typical of a Reflective Explorer pattern, where self-revision is dense and the human rhythm is recognisable. Combinations of lower HI with higher CTF and RMD, when they appear, tend to move closer to smoother, more machine-like patterns that are characteristic of the Evidence Weaver side of the profile.
